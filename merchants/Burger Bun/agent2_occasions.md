**Date:** Recurring (can be posted anytime; recommended launch week: Feb 25 or any Week 2-5 slot)

The 18-35 audience includes early lunch breakers, remote workers, and deal-seekers. Burger Bun opens early and most competitors don't push morning/pre-lunch promos -- this is a window to own. The "in-person or phone pickup only" angle also creates exclusivity (not available on delivery apps), which drives urgency and FOMO.
**Suggested campaign angle:** "The 10AM move that saves you 15%." -- Position the Early Bird Special as the insider hack for regulars. Hero a loaded tray (Boss Burger + fries, or a breakfast-adjacent item) with the time and discount front and center. Lean into the brand's confident "if you know, you know" energy. ## Strategic Notes

**Recurring Themes Across the 30 Days:**
- **Game Day Energy (4 posts):** NBA weekend, Selection Sunday, First Four, First Round -- builds a sustained "Burger Bun = game day food" narrative
- **March Madness Arc (3 posts):** Selection Sunday teaser --> First Four bridge --> First Round payoff. This mirrors the pre-occasion / day-of / continuation framework from the campaign guidelines.
- **Counter-Programming (2 posts):** Chili Day and Potato Chip Day -- these show the brand's confident, playful personality by saying "our thing is better than that thing"
- **Menu Discovery (3 posts):** Tortilla Chip Day (Mexican menu reveal), Cold Cuts Day (pastrami authority moment), and Carne Asada Torta (pure product spotlight) expand what followers associate with Burger Bun beyond just burgers
- **Happy Hour / Snack (1 post):** National Snack Day directly addresses the merchant's "happy hour / early bird" priority
- **Early Bird Special (1 recurring post):** 10AM-11AM, 15% off everything, in-person/phone pickup only. Drives morning foot traffic and creates an exclusive "if you know, you know" deal that's not on delivery apps

**Early Bird Special (Recurring):** The merchant is running a 10AM-11AM Early Bird Special -- 15% off everything, in-person only or phone pickup. This is a recurring promo that can be posted anytime to drive morning foot traffic and phone orders.

**Tone Consistency:** Every occasion is filtered through Burger Bun's "Playful. Confident. Obsessed." voice. No generic "Happy [Holiday]!" posts -- every occasion has a specific menu item hook and a clear ordering CTA.
