## Bio Audit

### What's Working
- **Menu categories listed** -- tells visitors what to expect immediately
- **Phone number included** -- useful for direct orders
- **Address included** -- good for local SEO
- **Hours mentioned** (7 days a week) -- reduces friction
- **Content manager credited** (@karacontent) -- shows professionalism

### What's Missing / SEO Issues
1. **No Instagram category set** -- should be "Restaurant" or "Fast Casual Restaurant" (IG category shows in search results)
2. **No website/Linktree link** -- massive missed opportunity for online ordering, DoorDash storefront, or menu
3. **No DoorDash/delivery mention** -- no CTA driving orders
4. **Generic food list** -- "Burgers | Sandwiches | Tortas" is descriptive but not searchable or differentiating
5. **No unique value prop** -- what makes Burger Bun different? (Award-winning, hand-pressed Angus beef, since 2008)
6. **No branded hashtag** -- no #BurgerBunLA or similar for UGC tracking
7. **No keyword-rich language** -- Instagram bio text is indexed by IG search; missing terms like "Glendale food," "best burgers Glendale"
8. **Address formatting** -- no line break between phone and hours, slightly cluttered
