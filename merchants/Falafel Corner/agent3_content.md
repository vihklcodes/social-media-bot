# Agent 3: Nano Banana Pro Content -- Falafel Corner (@falafelcorner_main)

**Coverage Period:** February 18 -- March 20, 2026
**Total Occasions:** 14 unique occasions + 2 recurring hooks (Falafel Friday, Meatless Monday)
**Brand Voice Evolution:** Templated, Generic, Promotional (current) --> Confident. Fresh. Sacramento's Own. (target)
**Caption Formula (NEW):** Scroll-stopping hook --> line break --> 2-4 punchy body lines --> line break --> "Order on DoorDash -- link in bio!" CTA --> line break --> emoji bookends
**Brand Colors:** Deep Red/Maroon (#AD2429), Terracotta Orange CTA (#D2482B), White (#FFFFFF)
**Playwright-Verified Environment Colors:** Primary flyer background: Warm amber/burnt orange gradient (#b05e03 to #a33004) | Secondary: Warm beige/cream (#fbe2ba to #ffebc6) | Deep crimson (#660711 to #910111) | Brand red (#AD2429) | Brand orange CTA (#D2482B) | Food center tones: Golden-brown (#d2a586 to #bb8249)
**Photography Style:** Studio-quality food hero on designed gradient backgrounds. 60% 45-degree views, 30% overhead flat bowl shots, 10% eye-level. Food centered and fully contained (NOT edge-cropped). Ultra crisp, high-saturation, warm-toned.
**Typography System:** Oswald Medium (tall, condensed, geometric sans-serif with uniform stroke width, vertically elongated, authoritative -- ALL CAPS) for headlines | Bebas Neue (ultra-condensed, tall, all-caps display font, cinematic poster feel, uniform stroke weight) for product names | DM Sans SemiBold (clean, geometric sans-serif with slightly rounded terminals, modern, approachable) for CTAs/body text
**Text Overlay Treatment:** Always used. Heavy headline style. Z-pattern default with headline at top, food hero center, ORDER NOW CTA at bottom. White text on warm gradient backgrounds -- no gradient overlays needed. Falafel Corner flame logo at top-left in ~70% of posts.
**Serving Style:** White ceramic bowls/plates in designed flyers; clear plastic catering containers in real photos
**Decorative Elements:** Line-drawn food sketches (falafel outlines, pita shapes) at ~30% opacity; fresh herb/vegetable garnishes scattered around food hero
**Environment Note:** Falafel Corner's Instagram is almost entirely AI-generated promotional flyers, NOT photos from real restaurant interiors. The "environment" is the designed flyer aesthetic: warm amber/orange gradient backgrounds, studio-quality food imagery, floating food on gradient backgrounds. Some prompts in this campaign introduce real-environment elements (food on actual counters/tables) to evolve the brand toward more authentic content while maintaining the brand color palette.

### Tone Evolution Strategy
The current brand voice is a 3/10 energy level with zero personality. This campaign introduces:
1. **Counter-programming confidence** (Occasions 3, 4, 10): "Respectfully... pita chips clear"
2. **Sacramento local pride** (Occasions 5, 11, 13A): "Light the beam. Order the food."
3. **Cultural awareness and respect** (Occasions 1, 8, 13B): "Eid Mubarak. Happy Nowruz. Halal. Always."
4. **Engagement hooks** (Occasions 7, 9): "Rank these" / "argue in the comments"
5. **Menu education** (Occasions 3, 6, 7): "Did you know we have chipotle hummus?"
6. **Value propositions** (Occasions 4, 14): "$17.50 -- that's two meals"

The shift should feel like Falafel Corner hired a real social media manager who actually eats there -- not like the brand personality changed overnight. Every post still says "Falafel Corner" and still uses the warm amber/crimson/beige palette. The upgrade is in the captions (personality, CTAs, line breaks, engagement hooks) and the prompt variety (four layout patterns instead of one).

### Key Performance Indicators to Watch
- **Caption CTAs added:** 0/post (current) --> 1/post (new). This is the single biggest conversion fix.
- **Hashtag count:** 3-6/post (current) --> 18-20/post (new). Should improve discoverability immediately.
- **Halal mentions:** 0 Instagram captions mention halal (current) --> 9 of 16 (new). The #1 customer-cited differentiator in DoorDash reviews, completely absent from social media.
- **Sacramento references:** 0 location-specific mentions (current) --> 7 of 16 (new). Local relevance for local discovery.
- **Engagement hooks (questions/challenges):** 0 (current) --> 6 of 16 (new). The feed currently asks the audience nothing.
- **Layout variety:** 1 pattern (current Z-pattern only) --> 4 patterns (Z-Pattern, Golden Ratio Split, Asymmetric Hero, Food-Framed Center). Visual variety prevents feed fatigue.

### The Biggest Opportunity
Falafel Corner has 1,806 weekly DoorDash orders but only 133 Instagram followers. The gap between market traction and social presence is the largest in the entire merchant dataset. Every occasion post includes a DoorDash ordering CTA because the audience is already delivery-first. The goal is not to change behavior -- it is to put a brand personality behind the orders they are already making. If even 1% of their weekly DoorDash customers follow the Instagram, that is 18 new followers per week -- doubling the current count in under 2 months.
