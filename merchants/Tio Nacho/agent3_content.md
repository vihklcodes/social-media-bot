## Design Philosophy

All prompts are written to produce **editorial-quality** food and lifestyle photography with professional graphic design — not basic Canva templates. Key principles:

- **Textured backgrounds** (aged stucco, rustic wood grain, brushed concrete) — never flat solid colors
- **Atmospheric lighting** (warm key light, soft shadows, depth) — never flat/even illumination
- **Sensory food language** (steam, cheese pulls, glistening sauce, crispy edges) — never generic "food on plate"
- **Dynamic typography** (bold with subtle shadow/glow, layered into the scene) — never plain text pasted on
- **Mood-first composition** (the image should feel like somewhere you want to be)

Brand colors (from Agent 1): Lime green #5AA534, Brown wood #6B4423, Orange CTA #FF6201, Red accent #E53935, Yellow-orange #FFB900. Typography: Bree Serif (headlines), Poppins (body/CTA).
