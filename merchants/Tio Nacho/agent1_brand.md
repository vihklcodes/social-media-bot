### Caption Length
**Highly variable:**

- **Medium (3-5 lines):** Typical event announcements with details
- **Long (8-10 lines):** Operational announcements (holiday hours), community messages

### Hook Style (from real first lines)
1. **Emoji-first energy hook:** "✨ #nightlife #thepeopleschoice..." (leads with sparkle emoji + hashtag vibe)
2. **Direct announcement:** "HAPPY HOLIDAYS TO YOU ALL FROM US AT TIO NACHO"
3. **Event CTA:** "Stop by for a drink tomorrow Friday 11th!"

**Pattern:** NO consistent hook formula. Some posts lead with emojis, some with all-caps announcements, some with casual CTAs. The brand does NOT use "scroll-stopping questions" or "curiosity gaps" — hooks are straightforward and informational.

### Body Structure
**Event announcements:** List format with details (date, time, entertainment lineup, pricing)

**Example:**

**Community messages:** Gratitude + team appreciation

**Example:**

**Operational announcements:** Straightforward informational text with details in list format

**Example:**
> "To allow our team to spend time with loved ones, we will be closing:
> Wednesday 12/24 @ 7PM
> Thursday 12/25 ALL DAY
> Friday 12/26 Reopen @ 9AM"

### Emoji Usage
**Heavy and integrated throughout captions.** Emojis are NOT just decorative — they function as bullet points, emphasis markers, and tone indicators.

**Patterns:**
- Food emojis for menu items: 🌮🍻🌯🌵

**Example:** "🥊💥" before event name, "🌮🍻" after venue mention, "🎧🗣️🥂" as bullet points for vendor list

### Line Break Structure
**Varies by post type:**
- **Event announcements:** One element per line (date, venue, entertainment, etc.)
- **Community messages:** Dense short paragraphs, 2-3 sentences per line
- **Hashtag groups:** All hashtags in a single line or broken into 2 lines

**Varies:**
- **End of caption (most common):** Hashtags appear after all body text, either inline or on new line
- **Integrated in opening line:** Example: "✨ #nightlife #thepeopleschoice #vidadenoche..."
