## Bio Audit

### What's Working
- **Heritage/founding year** -- "since 1970" is a strong trust signal (56 years!)
- **Multi-location mentioned** -- "7 locations to serve you"
- **Signature dish referenced** -- #barnyardbuster in hashtags (their famous breakfast)
- **Address in IG address field** -- set for primary location
- **Location hashtags** -- #centralohio #newarkohio #zanesville help regional discovery

### What's Missing / SEO Issues
1. **Bio is a single run-on line** -- no structure, no line breaks, hard to scan on mobile
2. **Hashtags in the bio text** -- hashtags in the bio body look messy and waste character space. They belong in posts
3. **No food description** -- "Down Home Cooking" is vague. What do they actually serve? Breakfast? Comfort food?
4. **No IG category set** -- should be "American Restaurant" or "Breakfast & Brunch Restaurant"
5. **No phone number** -- no way to contact directly from bio
6. **No website link** -- no menu, no online ordering, nothing
7. **No hours** -- when are they open?
8. **No CTA** -- nothing driving orders, visits, or engagement
9. **No DoorDash mention** -- despite 951 weekly MP orders
10. **Display name uses all 30 characters** -- good, but could swap apostrophe placement for search optimization
11. **Following > Followers** -- 1,111 following vs 1,029 followers suggests follow-for-follow
12. **"Barnyard Buster" hidden in hashtag** -- their most famous menu item is buried as a hashtag instead of featured prominently
