# Agent 2: Occasion Strategist -- Tee Jaye's Country Place

**Date:** 2026-02-19
**Campaign Window:** February 20 -- March 21, 2026 (30 days)
**Frequency:** 3 posts per week
**Total Occasions:** 12

**Merchant-Specified Campaigns:**
1. Lenten Friday specials (limited to items with available photos: Fisherman's Platter, Fried Fish Sandwich)
2. Corned Beef & Cabbage for St. Patrick's Day
3. Content that goes BEYOND the Barnyard Buster (with one dedicated Buster spotlight)

**Key Context:** Lent began Feb 18 (Ash Wednesday). Lent runs through April 2. Every Friday during Lent is a fish/seafood day for observant Christians -- Tee Jaye's has Lenten Specials. We have photos for the Fisherman's Platter and the Fried Fish Sandwich, so Lenten content will feature these two items across two Fridays.

## Week 1: February 20-26

### Occasion 1: Lenten Friday #1 -- "Friday Fish Fry at Tee Jaye's"
**Date:** Friday, February 20
**Why it fits:** First Lenten Friday content. Tee Jaye's has Lenten specials on the website. Central Ohio has a strong Catholic/Christian community -- Lenten fish fry is a massive dining-out tradition in the Midwest. This is a merchant-confirmed priority.
**Campaign angle:** Launch the Lenten series. Feature the Fisherman's Platter as the hero. "Every Friday through Easter" creates a recurring appointment.
**Hero food item:** Fisherman's Platter (golden fried pollack, popcorn shrimp, fries, coleslaw, Tee Jaye's sauce)

### Occasion 2: National Pancake Day -- "Pancake Breakfast at Tee Jaye's"
**Date:** Wednesday, February 25
**Why it fits:** Tee Jaye's serves breakfast all day and has a dedicated Pancakes, Waffles & French Toast section. This is a natural fit that showcases the breakfast menu BEYOND the Barnyard Buster. National Pancake Day trends heavily on social media -- easy hashtag ride.
**Campaign angle:** "You know us for the Barnyard Buster, but have you had our pancake breakfast?" Highlight the classic pancake stack alongside eggs and bacon. Position as a saveable/shareable "what to order" post. Breakfast served all day messaging.
**Hero food item:** Pancake breakfast (classic stack of pancakes with eggs and bacon)

### Occasion 3: National Chili Day -- "Warm Up with Our Homemade Chili"
**Date:** Thursday, February 26
**Why it fits:** Tee Jaye's makes homemade chili (confirmed from their Instagram reel about soups). Late February in Ohio is peak cold-weather comfort food season. Chili is a high-margin, easy-to-promote item. This showcases a lunch/dinner offering beyond breakfast.
**Campaign angle:** "Made from scratch, just like Granny would." Feature the chili as part of the homemade soups lineup (chicken & noodles, pinto bean, vegetable, chili). Cold weather + comfort food = immediate craving trigger.
**Hero food item:** Bowl of homemade chili with cornbread or crackers

## Week 2: February 27 -- March 5

### Occasion 4: Lenten Friday #2 -- "Fish Sandwich Friday"
**Date:** Friday, February 27
**Why it fits:** Second Lenten Friday. Rotate the featured Lenten item to keep the series fresh. The Fried Fish Sandwich is a classic diner Lenten staple with broad appeal.
**Campaign angle:** Spotlight the Fried Fish Sandwich as this week's Lenten feature. "Every Friday, a new reason to come in for Lent." Keep the Lenten series momentum going with a different hero item.
**Hero food item:** Fried Fish Sandwich (crispy fried fish on a bun with tartar sauce)

### Occasion 5: Winter Menu Spotlight -- "Warm Turtle Brownie Sundae"
**Date:** Monday, March 2
**Why it fits:** It's still peak winter in Ohio and Tee Jaye's winter menu has a showstopper dessert that most people don't know about. Dessert content performs extremely well on Instagram (high save and share rates). This is a surprise-and-delight moment -- "wait, a country diner makes THIS?"
**Campaign angle:** "It's still cold out. Warm up the right way." Feature the Warm Turtle Brownie Sundae as an indulgent winter treat. Warm chocolate brownie iced with caramel, pecans and Belgian chocolate curls accompanied by vanilla ice cream, caramel drizzle, whipped topping and chopped nuts. Lean into the contrast -- cozy comfort diner meets decadent dessert.
**Hero food item:** Warm Turtle Brownie Sundae (warm chocolate brownie, caramel, pecans, Belgian chocolate curls, vanilla ice cream, caramel drizzle, whipped topping, chopped nuts)

### Occasion 6: National Cold Cuts Day -- "Turkey Bacon Swiss & The Reuben"
**Date:** Tuesday, March 3
**Why it fits:** Tee Jaye's has standout sandwich options that deserve the spotlight. The Grilled Turkey Bacon & Swiss and the Reuben are both hearty, photogenic, lunch-driving items. Cold Cuts Day is a hook to talk about sandwiches broadly. "We don't just do cold cuts -- we do HOT sandwiches."
**Campaign angle:** Counter-programming: "Forget cold cuts. We're going HOT." Feature the Grilled Turkey Bacon & Swiss and the Reuben side by side. Two sandwiches, one post -- positions Tee Jaye's as a lunch destination, not just breakfast.
**Hero food item:** Grilled Turkey Bacon & Swiss sandwich + Reuben sandwich (dual hero)

## Week 3: March 6-12

### Occasion 7: Comfort Bowl Season -- "Southern Fried Steak Bites Mashed Potato Bowl"
**Date:** Saturday, March 7
**Why it fits:** This is one of Tee Jaye's most craveable winter menu items and it's basically a hug in a bowl. Mashed potato bowls are trending on food Instagram. Six bite-size Southern fried beefsteak bites, sausage gravy, mashed potatoes, corn, green beans, topped with shredded cheddar. It's the ultimate comfort food for a cold March Saturday.
**Campaign angle:** "One bowl. Everything you need." Showcase this loaded comfort bowl as the go-to winter weekend order. Highlight every layer -- the fried steak bites, the gravy, the cheese on top. This is saveable content for anyone who sees it and immediately needs it.
**Hero food item:** Southern Fried Steak Bites Mashed Potato Bowl (six Southern fried beefsteak bites, Tee Jaye's sausage gravy, mashed potatoes, corn, green beans, shredded cheddar cheese)

### Occasion 8: The Classic -- "The Barnyard Buster"
**Date:** Tuesday, March 10
**Why it fits:** It's the signature. The no-brainer. The one everyone already knows. Instead of avoiding the Buster, lean ALL the way in for one dedicated post. Their feed is dominated by it for a reason -- it's iconic. Give it its hero moment so the rest of the campaign can spotlight everything else.
**Campaign angle:** "You already know. The Barnyard Buster. The one you've been thinking about since the last time you had it." No complicated hook needed -- just the classic, front and center. This is the loyalty play: remind existing fans why they love it, and make new followers curious enough to try it.
**Hero food item:** The Barnyard Buster (the signature dish, piled high)

### Occasion 9: Oatmeal Nut Waffles Day -- Waffle & French Toast Spotlight
**Date:** Wednesday, March 11
**Why it fits:** Tee Jaye's has a full Pancakes, Waffles & French Toast section. This is another "beyond the Buster" breakfast spotlight in the campaign. Waffles are highly visual and photogenic -- the grid pattern, butter pooling in squares, syrup drizzle. Great for engagement (polls: "pancakes or waffles?").
**Campaign angle:** "Pancakes had their day (literally). Now it's Waffle Wednesday." Create a waffle vs. pancakes debate post or poll. Show the full sweet breakfast spread -- waffles, French toast, pancakes -- to reinforce that Tee Jaye's is more than eggs and biscuits.
**Hero food item:** Belgian waffle with butter and syrup (or French toast stack)

## Week 4: March 13-21

### Occasion 10: Selection Sunday -- March Madness Group Order
**Date:** Sunday, March 15
**Why it fits:** Selection Sunday kicks off March Madness. Columbus, Ohio is a huge college sports market (Ohio State). Group watching = group ordering. Tee Jaye's has appetizers, platters, and carry-out -- this positions them as the "order for the crew" destination via DoorDash.
**Campaign angle:** "Your bracket's busted but dinner doesn't have to be. Order Tee Jaye's for the crew." Push carry-out and DoorDash delivery. Feature a spread: appetizers, sandwiches, the works. Drive group orders.
**Hero food item:** Multi-item spread (appetizers, sandwich platters -- group order showcase)

### Occasion 11: St. Patrick's Day -- Corned Beef & Cabbage Special
**Date:** Tuesday, March 17
**Why it fits:** Merchant-confirmed campaign. Tee Jaye's is running a corned beef and cabbage special for St. Patrick's Day. Central Ohio has a strong Irish-American community. This is a tentpole occasion -- every restaurant in Columbus will be posting St. Patrick's Day content, so Tee Jaye's needs to be in the mix.
**Campaign angle:** "Down Home Cookin' goes Irish. Corned Beef & Cabbage, Tee Jaye's style." Lean into the crossover between Irish comfort food and Tee Jaye's country comfort identity. Include pricing and availability.
**Hero food item:** Corned Beef & Cabbage plate (sliced corned beef, cabbage, potatoes, carrots)

### Occasion 12: First Day of Spring -- Country Comfort Meets Spring
**Date:** Saturday, March 21
**Why it fits:** Seasonal transition. After a long Ohio winter, the first day of spring is a feel-good moment. Tee Jaye's can bridge winter comfort food with spring energy. Also pushes "come visit us" messaging after being cooped up all winter.
**Campaign angle:** "Spring is here, but Granny's cookin' is timeless." Highlight lighter options (salads, wraps) alongside the classics. Drive foot traffic: "Come in and celebrate. Windows are open, coffee's fresh." Can also tease upcoming spring/Easter specials.
**Hero food item:** Country Chicken Tender Salad (lighter option for spring) or Breakfast Wrap (TJ Scramble Wrap)
