## Bio Audit

### What's Working
- **Hours are front and center** -- smart for a takeout-heavy spot
- **Phone number included** -- good for direct orders
- **DoorDash and Grubhub mentioned** -- shows delivery availability
- **Catering/Takeout/Delivery listed** -- covers all order modes
- **Address in IG address field** -- good for local discovery
- **Extensive highlights** -- 11 highlight categories shows effort (bilingual: Spanish/English)

### What's Missing / SEO Issues
1. **No cuisine description at all** -- nothing says Dominican, Puerto Rican, Caribbean, Latin, or any food type. A new visitor has zero idea what La Cocina serves
2. **Display name is extremely generic** -- "La Cocina" is one of the most common restaurant names. Needs location or cuisine qualifier
3. **No IG category set** -- should be "Latin American Restaurant" or "Caribbean Restaurant"
4. **"#️⃣1️⃣ and Only !" is confusing** -- unclear what this means. Is it a ranking claim? A uniqueness statement? Wastes valuable bio space
5. **No signature dishes** -- mofongo, pernil, pica pollo are highly searchable terms being missed
6. **No website link** -- despite having "Order online" highlight, no link in bio
7. **Formatting inconsistencies** -- "8:am-8:30pm" has a random colon, "⏱" and "⏰" used for same purpose, pipe separators in "Grubhub|Doordash" look unpolished
8. **🧰 toolbox emoji** -- doesn't relate to food delivery apps at all
9. **Hours formatting is messy** -- inconsistent spacing and punctuation
10. **No bilingual bio** -- feed is bilingual but bio is English-only (audience is likely heavily Spanish-speaking)
