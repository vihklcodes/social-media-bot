# La Cocina ‚Äî Brand Replication Blueprint

## 1. Brand Snapshot

La Cocina Tropical is a Dominican and Puerto Rican restaurant in the heart of Pennsauken, NJ's Hispanic community, serving bold, homestyle Latin Caribbean cuisine for 5+ years. Known for generous portions of mofongo, pernil, pica pollo, rotisserie chicken, and a rotating steam table. The brand identity is warm, loud, family-driven, and proudly bilingual (English/Spanish). Their Instagram is personality-driven ‚Äî mixing promotional food posts with behind-the-scenes humor featuring staff member "Nia" and the owner. The vibe is "your neighborhood spot that feels like home" with a no-frills, come-as-you-are energy. They also run a catering operation and recently partnered with Afterpay.

## 3. Visual System Blueprint

**A. Overall Aesthetic Category:**
- **Primary:** Bold & colorful (high-contrast promotional graphics with heavy text)
- **Secondary:** UGC-heavy (casual behind-the-scenes reels, iPhone-shot content)

**B. Color Palette (observed from actual posts):**
- Dominant: Dark backgrounds (black/charcoal #23212B) for promotional static posts, warm food browns (#A87154, #6B4E3A)
- Accent: Brand green (#067A00), brand orange (#FE741B), white text overlays
- Background style: Solid dark backgrounds for promo graphics; natural restaurant interior for BTS content
- Feed color consistency: VARIED ‚Äî no consistent color grid. Mix of dark promotional posts, light graphic posts, and natural-lit casual reels

**C. Image Composition Patterns (static photos ‚Äî last 3 months):**
- **Very few static photos** ‚Äî feed is ~75% Reels. Of the 12 most recent posts, only 3 are static photos (Posts 0, 1, 4)
- Camera angle: **45-degree dominant (~60%)** for food shots, with some overhead flat-lay for platters/spreads (~30%), and eye-level for interior/exterior (~10%)
- Close-up/wide ratio: ~50/50 ‚Äî close-ups of individual dishes AND wide shots of full spreads/platters
- Centered composition is dominant ‚Äî food placed center-frame
- **Edge-cropping:** Minimal ‚Äî most food is fully contained in frame. Platters and spreads occasionally extend to edges (~20% of food posts)
- Negative space: LOW ‚Äî images are filled with food, text overlays, or both
- People included: Yes ‚Äî frequently in Reels (owner, staff member Nia). Rarely in static photos
- **Surface/table details:** Food photographed on white styrofoam takeout containers, aluminum trays, or directly on steam table. No styled surfaces
- **Plate/serving ware:** White styrofoam containers (most common), aluminum catering trays, occasional white ceramic plates. Takeout-style presentation
- Carousel: Not observed in recent posts

## 5. Text Overlay Specifications

**Text frequency on images:** Always (on static photo posts) / Sometimes (reels have text in captions, not always overlaid on video)

**Text density:** Heavy headline style ‚Äî 3-6 text elements per promotional post (headline, sub-headline, phone number, website, CTA, logo)

**Font style observed on Instagram posts:**
- Headlines: Bold block sans-serif (consistent with Bebas Neue from website)
- Sub-text: Clean sans-serif (consistent with DM Sans)
- Phone number: Always included (856-324-0269) in promotional posts

**Font case:** ALL CAPS for headlines ("MOFONGO", "DELICIOUS DINNER SPECIALS", "LA COCINA"). Mixed case for sub-headlines and body text.

**Font weight:** Bold / Extra Bold for headlines. Medium for body/sub-headlines.

**Font size relative to image:** Headlines span ~60-70% of image width. Very large and dominant. Sub-headlines ~30-40% of width.

**Text alignment:** Centered dominant (~70% of text-overlay posts). Left-aligned for body text in some posts (~30%).

**Text color:**
- White text on dark backgrounds (most common)
- Yellow/gold text for accent words ("MOFONGO" in yellow on Mofongo post)
- Green (#067A00) for brand text
- Orange (#FE741B) for CTA/accent text

**Text Placement MAP:**
- **Top of image:** ~40% of text-overlay posts (headline + logo at top)
- **Center of image:** ~30% (text integrated with food imagery)
- **Bottom of image:** ~30% (CTA, phone number, website at bottom)
- **Multi-zone:** Most promotional posts have text at BOTH top AND bottom (headline top, CTA/phone bottom), with food hero in center
- Posts 0, 4: Headline at top-left, phone/CTA at bottom
- The brand does NOT default to bottom-only text placement

**Text Background Treatment MAP:**
- **No background treatment (raw text on image):** ~50% ‚Äî bold white/yellow text directly over dark food photos or dark backgrounds. Text weight carries readability.
- **Solid dark background:** ~30% ‚Äî some posts use a fully dark/black background as the canvas, making text treatment unnecessary
- **Semi-transparent overlay:** ~10% ‚Äî occasional gradient or tinted overlay
- **Brand color block:** ~10% ‚Äî orange or green accent boxes behind specific CTAs

**Text decoration:**
- La Cocina circular logo placed in corner of most promotional posts
- Phone number icon (üìû) used frequently
- Occasional emoji in overlays (üî•, üìç)
- No frames, borders, or sticker elements

## 8. Caption Architecture Guide

**Caption length:** SHORT ‚Äî typically 1-4 lines for static posts. Slightly longer (4-8 lines) for reels with storytelling. Not paragraph-heavy.

**Hook style:** Mix of:
- Question hooks: "Dinner time ready?", "Looking for catering that impresses before the meeting even starts?"
- Bold statement hooks: "South Jersey Dominican food done RIGHT üá©üá¥üî•"
- Command/CTA hooks: "Come try our MOFONGO", "Huddle up! üèà"
- Personal/casual hooks: "Another Nia work day well sort of üòÇ"

**Body structure:** Direct and promotional. Lists menu items, includes practical info (phone number, address). Occasionally tells a micro-story (food blogger style for the reel from external creator). No long-form storytelling from the brand itself.

**Emoji usage:** Moderate ‚Äî 2-5 per post. Used inline (not as bullet points). Common emojis: üî• üèà üåπ ‚ù§Ô∏è üìç üò≠ üíÄ üá©üá¥ üöß. Emojis placed after phrases, not at line beginnings.

**Line break structure:** Short lines, single-spaced. Not double-spaced. No bullet-point formatting.

**CTA style:** Practical and direct:

**Hashtag placement:** End of caption (after line break in longer posts). Not in first comment.

**Recurring phrases/patterns:**
