## Week 1: Feb 24 – Mar 1

### Occasion 1: National Tortilla Chip Day — Super Nachos Showcase
**Date:** Tuesday, Feb 24
**Why it fits:** National Tortilla Chip Day is a direct product tie-in. Jalisco's Super Nachos are loaded with choice of meat, beans, pico de gallo, sour cream, jalapeños, and guacamole cheese sauce — one of their most visually impressive dishes. Reviews specifically call out the nachos as "amazing." Perfect scroll-stopping hero shot on day one.
**Campaign angle:** Hero food shot of the Super Nachos with a "National Tortilla Chip Day" tie-in. CTA to order for delivery/pickup. Engagement prompt: "What's your go-to nacho topping?"
**Post type:** Day-of main post (hero content)
**Revenue tie-in:** Direct order driver — Super Nachos as the featured item

### Occasion 2: National Chili Day — Chiles Rellenos Spotlight
**Date:** Thursday, Feb 26
**Why it fits:** National Chili Day (4th Thursday of February) aligns perfectly with Jalisco's Chiles Rellenos — one of their featured Reels. The chile pepper is central to Mexican cuisine and Jalisco's identity. Also serves as a heat/spice theme that appeals to food lovers.
**Campaign angle:** Close-up hero shot of Chiles Rellenos with "CHILES RELLENOS" headline. Educational angle — what chiles rellenos are (stuffed peppers, battered, fried). Bilingual touch: "National Chili Day? We've been celebrating chiles every day since Jalisco."
**Post type:** Day-of main post
**Revenue tie-in:** Menu item spotlight — drives orders for chiles rellenos and other chile-based dishes

### Occasion 3: Lenten Sunday — Seafood Weekend Specials
**Date:** Sunday, Mar 1
**Why it fits:** We're deep into Lent. For Mexican Catholic families, Lenten Fridays mean no meat — seafood is the tradition. By posting on Sunday, we're setting up the coming week's Lenten Friday cravings. Jalisco has a full Seafood menu category and their DoorDash storefront hero image features a shrimp dish prominently.
**Campaign angle:** Shrimp cocktail or shrimp plate hero shot. "Cuaresma calls for seafood" bilingual hook. Position Jalisco as THE Lenten seafood spot in Sacramento. CTA: Order seafood for this week's Lenten Friday.
**Post type:** Pre-occasion teaser (building anticipation for Lenten Friday)
**Revenue tie-in:** Drives seafood orders during Lent — historically one of the highest-volume periods for Mexican restaurants

## Week 2: Mar 3 – Mar 8

### Occasion 4: Taco Tuesday — Street Taco Spotlight
**Date:** Tuesday, Mar 3
**Why it fits:** Taco Tuesday is the #1 recurring food occasion on Instagram. Jalisco's Street Tacos are their #1 Most Liked item on DoorDash (78% rating, 51 reviews) at just $4.95. This is their proven bestseller and the most affordable entry point to the menu. Seven meat options (al pastor, asada, carnitas, grilled chicken, barbacoa, chorizo, ground beef) give visual variety.
**Campaign angle:** "TACO TUESDAY" in bold with a spread of different tacos. Highlight the $4.95 price point. "7 meats. One taco. Your move." Engagement prompt: "Which meat are you picking?"
**Post type:** Day-of main post (recurring weekly potential)
**Revenue tie-in:** Highest-volume item — $4.95 is an impulse-order price point. Drives both DoorDash and in-store traffic.

### Occasion 5: Birria Ramen Spotlight — Unique Fusion Dish
**Date:** Thursday, Mar 5
**Why it fits:** Birria Ramen is Jalisco's most unique menu item — a fusion of Mexican birria and Japanese ramen that virtually no other Mexican restaurant in Sacramento offers. This is the kind of dish that goes viral on food TikTok/Instagram. It combines rich birria broth with ramen noodles, tender shredded beef, and traditional Mexican toppings. For a 66-follower account trying to grow, a unique item like this is exactly what drives discovery and shares.
**Campaign angle:** Hero shot of Birria Ramen in a bowl with steam, noodles, shredded beef, and rich red broth. "BIRRIA RAMEN" as the bold headline. "Only at Jalisco." Position this as something you can't get anywhere else. CTA: Order now.
**Post type:** Day-of main post (discovery/shareability)
**Revenue tie-in:** Premium menu item that drives curiosity orders. High shareability = organic reach beyond existing followers.

### Occasion 6: International Women's Day — The Women of Jalisco
**Date:** Sunday, Mar 8
**Why it fits:** Jalisco is named after the Mexican state where Javier Ibarra's mother grew up. The restaurant's authentic recipes carry a maternal/family lineage. International Women's Day is the perfect occasion to honor the women behind the food — Javier's mother, grandmothers, and the female staff who make the restaurant run. This personal storytelling builds emotional connection and differentiates from generic food content.
**Campaign angle:** Warm, personal hero image featuring a signature dish that represents the family recipe tradition (enchiladas or menudo — dishes passed down through generations). "The hands that taught us to cook" or "Desde Jalisco, con amor de mamá." Storytelling caption about the maternal food legacy.
**Post type:** Day-of main post (emotional engagement)
**Revenue tie-in:** Brand affinity builder — not a direct sales push, but builds the emotional loyalty that drives long-term customer retention. Secondary CTA to bring mom/abuela to the restaurant.
