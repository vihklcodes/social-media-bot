## 1. Brand Snapshot

Jalisco Restaurant Authentic Mexican Food is a family-owned Sacramento restaurant run by Javier Ibarra, named after the Mexican state of Jalisco in Guadalajara where his mother grew up. Started as a catering business in 2022 before opening a brick-and-mortar location. The brand positions itself as affordable, authentic Mexican food with a strong family/community identity. The restaurant embraces Mexican cultural elements (Loteria nights, papel picado decor) and serves a wide menu spanning tacos, burritos, seafood cocktails, quesabirria, enchiladas, menudo, and breakfast items. The Instagram presence is very young (66 followers, 13 posts) and heavily Reel-focused with minimal caption effort — the brand voice and visual system are still forming, making this an ideal time to establish a professional but authentic visual identity.

## Design Recommendations for Agent 3

Given that Jalisco has only 66 followers and 13 posts (most with minimal effort), the AI-generated content represents an opportunity to **establish** a visual identity rather than replicate one. Agent 3 should:

1. **Use website typography as the foundation:** Bebas Neue (headlines, ALL CAPS) + Satoshi (body, sentence case) in brand red #EE2D35 and white
2. **Keep the dark wood table + white plate environment:** This is the one consistent visual across all sources — it IS the Jalisco look
3. **Incorporate Mexican cultural design elements:** Papel picado (already used in 1 post), warm earth tones, traditional color palette (red, green, gold, orange)
4. **Build a bilingual caption voice:** English primary with natural Spanish touches — this matches the existing content pattern
5. **Design for a small audience:** Posts should be shareable and saveable to drive follower growth, not just engagement from existing followers. Include location tags, discovery hashtags, and CTA to the DoorDash storefront.
6. **Lean into the family story:** Javier Ibarra, mother from Jalisco, started as catering, now a restaurant. This narrative differentiates from generic Mexican restaurants.
7. **Vary layout patterns:** Since there's no established template to match, Agent 3 has creative freedom to use all 4 layout patterns (Z-Pattern, Golden Ratio, Food-Framed Center, Asymmetric Hero) across the 12 occasions.
