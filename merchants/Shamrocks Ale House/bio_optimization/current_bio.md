## Bio Audit

### What's Working
- **Website link set** -- points to their DoorDash Online Ordering storefront (shamrocksalehouse.com)
- **Tone is on-brand** -- short, no-frills, casual. Matches the bartender-announcement voice from the brand blueprint

### What's Missing / SEO Issues
1. **Display name wastes SEO space** -- "Shamrocks" alone tells IG search nothing. Should include "Ale House," cuisine type, or location
2. **No IG category set** -- should be "Bar" or "Sports Bar" to show up in category-based search
3. **No address in IG address field** -- 7805 Temple Terrace Hwy, Tampa, FL 33637 is missing entirely. Critical for local discovery
4. **Bio is too vague** -- "food and sports" could describe any bar in America. No mention of wings, Irish pub, craft beer, or Temple Terrace
5. **No phone number** -- regulars and new customers can't call directly from the profile
6. **No hours listed** -- customers don't know when to show up
7. **No signature items mentioned** -- 94-cent wings, shepherd's pie, fish and chips are searchable terms that would drive discovery
8. **No differentiator** -- what makes Shamrocks different from every other sports bar? Irish-American identity, daily specials, community vibe
9. **No highlights** -- no saved Story highlights for menu, specials, events, trivia night, karaoke. Highlights build credibility for new visitors
10. **No location in bio text** -- "Temple Terrace" is a key local search term completely missing
11. **"Come out and enjoy" is passive** -- doesn't tell anyone what they'll find or why they should care
