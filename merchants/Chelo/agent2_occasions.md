## Week 1: Feb 24 – Mar 1

### Occasion 1: Pita Chips & Hummus Day (Feb 24, Tuesday)
**Date:** Tuesday, February 24
**Why it's relevant:** National Tortilla Chip Day falls on Feb 24 — Chelo's twist is pita chips + their scratch-made hummus trio (traditional, sundried tomato, avocado). Positions hummus as the superior chip dip and highlights Chelo's house-made sides that many customers don't know about. The "our version is better" angle drives engagement.
**Suggested campaign angle:** "Tortilla chips? Nah. We do pita chips + triple hummus 🤌✨" Feature their three hummus varieties fanned out with fresh-baked pita chips. CTA: "Which hummus are you starting with? Order your trio now — link in bio." Engagement play: poll in Stories asking "Traditional vs. Sundried Tomato vs. Avocado."

## Week 4: Mar 16 – 22

### Occasion 10: St. Patrick's Day — Persian Green (Mar 17, Tuesday)
**Date:** Tuesday, March 17
**Why it's relevant:** St. Patrick's Day — actual date lands on Tuesday, perfect alignment. Creative Persian twist: green is everywhere in Persian cuisine — fresh herbs (sabzi), pistachio ice cream, green garnishes, herb-loaded dishes. This makes a seemingly off-brand holiday fun and shareable.
**Suggested campaign angle:** "St. Patrick's Day? We've been cooking green since day one 🍀🌿 Pistachios, herbs, and saffron — Persian green hits different." Feature: pistachio ice cream, herb-loaded plates, green garnishes. Fun, cheeky tone. CTA: "Celebrate with us — open 11-8. Order now."

### Occasion 11: Nowruz — Sabzi Polo Mahi (Mar 19, Thursday)
**Date:** Thursday, March 19
**Why it's relevant:** Nowruz (Persian New Year) is Friday, March 20, 2026 — the vernal equinox. This is THE biggest cultural moment for Chelo's core Persian/Iranian-American audience. Posting the day before drives orders for the holiday. The hero product is **Sabzi Polo Mahi** (herbed rice with white fish) served with **KooKoo Sabzi** (Persian herb frittata) — a traditional Nowruz dish that Chelo only makes once a year during this time in March. The once-a-year scarcity creates massive FOMO and urgency.
**Suggested campaign angle:** "Once a year. Only for Nowruz. Sabzi Polo Mahi is back 🐟🌿✨" Feature the Sabzi Polo Mahi (herbed rice with seared white fish) alongside KooKoo Sabzi. Highlight that this is a limited-time Nowruz special — available only during Persian New Year. CTA: "Order before it's gone — (818) 809-2391. Link in bio."
