## Bio Audit

### What's Working
- **Halal Restaurant category set** -- good for search discovery in the halal niche
- **Hours included** -- Mon-Fri 11-8, Sat 11-5 (clear and specific)
- **Phone number** -- included for direct contact
- **Catering mention** -- aligns with their strong catering business (~50% of feed)
- **"Order Online Now!" CTA** -- good intent, but no link to back it up

### What's Missing / SEO Issues
1. **No website link** -- "Order Online Now!" CTA goes nowhere without a link in bio
2. **No cuisine description** -- "Freshly Served From Our Grill" is vague. What cuisine? Persian? Mediterranean? Kabobs?
3. **Display name wastes SEO space** -- "CHELO" alone doesn't help search. Should include cuisine or location keywords
4. **No signature dishes mentioned** -- kabobs, saffron rice, hummus, pita are searchable terms
5. **No differentiator** -- what makes Chelo special? 30+ years experience, Chef Saeed, Persian heritage
6. **Sparkle emojis are generic** -- ✨...✨ framing doesn't convey brand personality
7. **Pipe formatting is odd** -- "|Contact us for catering|" looks like a coding error
8. **No full address** -- only "Van Nuys, CA" with no street address
9. **Sunday hours missing** -- are they closed? Customers won't know
10. **No branded hashtag** -- no #TasteOfChelo for UGC
