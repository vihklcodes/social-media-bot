## Daily Engagement Routine (15 min/day)

| Time | Action | Duration |
|------|--------|----------|
| Morning | Like 20-30 posts from local hashtags (#CortezColorado, #MesaVerde, #FourCorners) | 5 min |
| Morning | Leave 5-10 genuine, specific comments on local accounts | 5 min |
| Evening | Reply to every comment/DM on Thai Cortez posts | 3 min |
| Evening | Like/comment on 5 stories from local accounts | 2 min |

**Comment quality > quantity.** Never write "looks great!" — write something specific:
- "That green chili burger looks unreal — do you guys do collabs?"
- "Cortez sunsets hit different. Perfect night for Thai food tbh"
- "We just did something similar with our Drunken Noodles last week, love seeing Cortez food scene grow"
