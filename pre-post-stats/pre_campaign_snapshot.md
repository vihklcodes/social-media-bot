- **Falafel Corner** (@falafelcorner_main) has **1,806 weekly DoorDash orders** but only **134 followers** and **0.7 avg likes** — the single biggest social-to-order disconnect in the dataset. 223 posts but almost all are AI-generated flyers with near-zero engagement. Last posted Oct 2025.
- **La Cocina** has grown from 8,598 to **8,739 followers** (+141) since initial data collection. Also grown from untracked to **2,293 total posts** — they post very frequently.
- **Tee Jayes** has the most concerning engagement: **1,591 posts** with an average of **2.2 likes per post** and almost zero comments. Despite having 1,029 followers, engagement is essentially dead.
- **Thai Cortez** has the highest per-follower engagement rate at **9.24%** — small but mighty. Their audience is genuinely engaged.
- **Burger Bun** hasn't posted since Jan 3, 2026 (~7 weeks inactive). All recent content is 100% video/Reels.

## Key Takeaways for Experiment Baseline

1. **Biggest opportunity:** Tee Jayes (0.23% engagement, 2.2 avg likes despite 1K followers) and Burger Bun (0.49% engagement despite professional agency content) — these have the most room to improve
2. **Most engaged per follower:** Thai Cortez (9.24%). Falafel Corner is the biggest untapped opportunity — 1,806 orders/wk with 134 followers and 0.7 avg likes.
3. **Highest absolute engagement:** Chelo (174.8 avg likes, 2.44% rate) and La Cocina (88.8 avg likes) — these are the "control" benchmarks to beat
4. **Posting frequency varies wildly:** La Cocina posts almost daily (2,293 posts), Falafel Corner has 223 posts but all AI flyers with zero traction, Thai Cortez has only 19 posts ever
